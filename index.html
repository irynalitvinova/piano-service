Piano Service

At Piano Service, our expert technicians have been tuning and repairing pianos since 1999 in Austin, Texas.

From concert grand pianos to historical upright home pianos, our technicians are able to help tune your instrument and offer expert advice to help maximise both performance and longevity.

With our quality reputation, we have offered more services to our loyal customers and now provide complete piano maintenance.
